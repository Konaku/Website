Founded in 2006, Konaku has a relatively long but quiet history. The Free Company was founded on August 26, 2013, by a group of friends on the Midgardsormr server. Since then, it's grown.

Konaku does not openly recruit, but is always welcoming new players. The group is proud to be a small FC that focuses on a strong relationship amongst its players.
