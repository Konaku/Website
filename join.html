Free Company

If you would like to join our Free Company, you can send a /tell to any member that is not Inactive or a New Recruit. You can find a list of members by clicking on the lodestone page.

Alternatively, you may also send an application from the sign board posted outside the House, located in The Goblet Ward 1, in the North Subdivision. We're in building lot #35.

Linkshell

Would you want to join our Linkshell instead, all you need to do is request an invitation from one of the Linkshell leaders. You can find a list of members by selecting the Linkshell page from the Lodestone drop down. We look forward to being your friend!
